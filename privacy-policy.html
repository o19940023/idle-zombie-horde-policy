Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2025

Qaf Studio ("we", "our", or "us") built the Idle Zombie Horde mobile application (the "App") as a free, ad-supported application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. The App does not require account creation or login.

However, the following non-personal data may be collected automatically through third-party services:

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • Usage data: App usage statistics, session duration, level progression, crash logs
  • Advertising data: Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase Analytics — for app analytics and crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Unity Analytics — for game performance monitoring. Unity Privacy Policy

3. Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and other technologies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your preferences and usage patterns.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Turn off Personalized Ads

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

5. Data Retention

Game progress data (such as current level) is stored locally on your device using Unity's PlayerPrefs system. This data is not transmitted to our servers. You can delete this data by uninstalling the App.

6. Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • Right to data portability

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
